About Me

I'm a Digitial Media student at Chattanooga State who is very passionate about photography and several other things.


I've been actively photographing for the past 4 years now and anticipate never slowing down. I actively travel to different cities and states in search for perfect or meaningful photographs.


Most of my photography focuses on documenting the human existence, whether it be on the streets of Chattanooga or in the metro tunnels of Atlanta, GA. I've actually embedded a video I was featured in a few months back with my friend Bobby in Atlanta.
