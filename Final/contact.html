Film Cameras

Dating back as far as the 1880's, film photography was the birth of photography. And it's no secret, the look and feel of film will stand the test of time. Whether it be for nostalgic or fashion purposes, film has made a comeback in recent years.


And because of the film boom, prices of film cameras have boomed as well. Kids these days are paying upwards of three big ones for an outdated and only slightly functional film camera.


Every kid in their pointy shoes wants to blab about the superior quality and colors of film, but in reality, the same quality and colors can be acheived with digital cameras. Not to mention, there are all these YouTubers with SquareSpace sponsorships hyping up the cameras and the price.
